I'm a film enthusiast and a regular Letterboxd user, and also a starting Web Developer. I want to combine my two passions and create a functional and good-loking app.
Popular on Letterboxd is a website where you can find the most viewed and liked films/film lists from the movie-enthusiast community of Letterboxd.com.
In the app, you will be able to select if you want to see the weekly or all-time most popular films/film lists from Letterboxd. Also, you can filter films by genre, and you can search for specific keywords when you're browsing the lists
